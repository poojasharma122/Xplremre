Las Vegas Itineraries to Maximise Your Visit

Planning your time in Las Vegas can feel overwhelming with so much to see and do. Whether you have a weekend or a full week, these carefully crafted itineraries offer a mix of experiences designed to help you make the most of your trip. From fast-paced city adventures to more relaxed exploration, there’s a plan to suit your style.

Classic Vegas Itinerary

Kick off your trip by immersing yourself in the iconic landmarks along the Strip. Spend your day visiting famous spots like the Bellagio fountains and the High Roller observation wheel. In the evening, enjoy a Cirque du Soleil show or a headline performance. On day two, explore Fremont Street’s buzzing atmosphere, catch the light show, and grab dinner at one of the downtown eateries. Wrap up with a night walk through the illuminated cityscape.

Party Itinerary

Start with pool parties at lively dayclubs, soaking up the sun with music and drinks. As night falls, hop between some of the city’s trendiest bars and nightclubs, hitting hotspots with DJ sets and rooftop views. Include stops at late-night eateries to recharge, and consider a VIP table for a true nightlife experience. This itinerary is ideal for night owls craving nonstop energy.

Relax and Recharge Itinerary

Begin with a morning yoga session or meditation class to ease into the day. Spend your afternoon at a quiet spa, enjoying massages and wellness treatments. Later, visit peaceful spots like the Bellagio Conservatory and Botanical Gardens. For evenings, opt for laid-back dinners in intimate settings and strolls along calm parts of the Strip or downtown, avoiding crowds.

Foodie Itinerary

Explore Las Vegas’s diverse food scene by starting with a gourmet breakfast at a renowned chef-owned restaurant. Spend your day sampling international cuisines from casual food trucks to upscale eateries. Book a tasting menu experience in the evening at one of the city’s top dining venues. Include stops at local markets or specialty coffee shops to get a true sense of the city’s culinary variety.

Family-Friendly Itinerary

Tailored for visitors with kids, this itinerary includes stops at interactive museums such as the Discovery Children’s Museum and the Marvel Avengers S.T.A.T.I.O.N. Explore the Shark Reef Aquarium and enjoy fun-filled afternoons at parks with splash pads and playgrounds. Include early-evening shows suitable for all ages and dinners at family-friendly restaurants with diverse menus.

Adventure and Outdoors Itinerary

Combine city thrills with nature’s beauty. Start your day with a hike or scenic drive in Red Rock Canyon. Back in the city, try adrenaline activities like indoor skydiving or ziplining across Fremont Street. Cap the day with a helicopter tour to see the dazzling city lights from above. This itinerary suits active travellers who want a blend of urban and outdoor excitement.

Arts and Culture Itinerary

Discover Las Vegas beyond the neon lights by visiting the Neon Museum to see historic signage. Spend time exploring galleries in the Downtown Arts District and catch live performances or independent theatre productions. Include a visit to the Mob Museum and end your day with a quiet meal in one of the city’s artsy neighborhoods.

Budget-Friendly Itinerary

Experience Las Vegas without overspending. Start with free attractions like the Bellagio fountains and the Wildlife Habitat at the Flamingo. Explore the Fremont Street Experience and take advantage of happy hour deals at bars and eateries. Look for discounted show tickets and consider using public transport to save on travel costs. This itinerary proves you don’t need to splurge to enjoy the city.
